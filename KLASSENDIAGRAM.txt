RidecountSystem
  owns PluginConfig
  owns YamlRidecountStorage
  creates RidecountService
  registers RidecountSignListener
  registers TrainCartsApiBridge
  registers RidecountCommand

RidecountSignListener
  depends on RidecountService
  uses RidecountSignDetector

TrainCartsApiBridge
  depends on RidecountService
  uses RidecountSignDetector

RidecountService
  depends on RidecountStorage

RidecountStorage
  implemented by YamlRidecountStorage

RidecountCommand
  depends on RidecountStorage
